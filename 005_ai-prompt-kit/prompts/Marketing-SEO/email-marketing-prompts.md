# Email Marketing Prompts

> Free prompts for email sequences, campaigns, and copywriting. Sources: [Atlassian](https://www.atlassian.com/blog/artificial-intelligence/ai-prompts-for-marketing), [OptiMonk](https://www.optimonk.com/chatgpt-prompts-for-marketing/), [AirOps](https://www.airops.com/prompts/free-marketing-prompts-ai)

## Welcome Sequences

### New Subscriber Welcome Series
```
Design a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers interested in [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

For each email provide:
- Email number and timing (days after signup)
- Subject line (under 50 characters)
- Preview text
- Main message/theme
- Key CTA
- Tone: [friendly/professional/casual]

Goals: Build trust, educate about value, drive first conversion
```

### SaaS Trial Welcome Sequence
```
Create a drip campaign for new trial users of our [SaaS PRODUCT].

Structure:
- Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + quick start guide
- Email 2 (Day 2): Key feature highlight
- Email 3 (Day 5): Success story/social proof
- Email 4 (Day 10): Overcome common objections
- Email 5 (Day 13): Conversion push with offer

Include subject lines and key points for each.
```

## Campaign Emails

### Product Launch Email
```
Write a product launch email for [PRODUCT NAME].

Details:
- Product: [DESCRIPTION]
- Key benefits: [BENEFIT 1, 2, 3]
- Target audience: [AUDIENCE]
- Launch offer: [DISCOUNT/BONUS]
- Urgency element: [DEADLINE/SCARCITY]

Include:
- Attention-grabbing subject line
- Preview text
- Opening hook
- Benefits (not features)
- Social proof element
- Clear CTA
- P.S. line
```

### Re-engagement Campaign
```
Create a 3-email re-engagement sequence for subscribers who haven't opened emails in 60+ days.

Email 1: "We miss you" + value reminder
Email 2: Exclusive offer to come back
Email 3: Last chance before removal

For each include:
- Subject line (curiosity-driven)
- Emotional hook
- Clear value proposition
- Single CTA
```

### Abandoned Cart Sequence
```
Design a 3-email abandoned cart sequence for our [E-COMMERCE STORE].

Timing: 1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours after abandonment

Include for each:
- Subject line
- Main message
- Objection handling
- Incentive progression (if any)
- Urgency elements

Product context: [PRODUCT TYPE, PRICE RANGE]
```

## Subject Lines

### Subject Line Generator
```
Generate 15 email subject lines for [EMAIL PURPOSE].

Include a mix of:
- Curiosity-driven (open loops)
- Benefit-focused
- Urgency/scarcity
- Personal/conversational
- Question-based
- Number/list-based

Keep under 50 characters. Avoid spam triggers.
Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
```

### A/B Test Subject Lines
```
I'm sending an email about [TOPIC] to [AUDIENCE].

Create 5 pairs of subject lines to A/B test, varying:
- Pair 1: Question vs Statement
- Pair 2: Personal vs Formal
- Pair 3: Short vs Long
- Pair 4: Emoji vs No emoji
- Pair 5: Benefit vs Curiosity
```

## Newsletter Content

### Weekly Newsletter Structure
```
Create a template structure for our weekly [INDUSTRY] newsletter.

Sections to include:
- Opening hook/personal note
- Main feature article summary
- Quick tips/insights (3-5)
- Tool or resource of the week
- Community highlight or question
- CTA section

Tone: [TONE]
Length: Scannable, 500-700 words total
```

### Newsletter Topic Ideas
```
Generate 12 newsletter topic ideas for [BUSINESS TYPE] targeting [AUDIENCE].

For each provide:
- Main theme
- Potential subject line
- 3 sub-topics to cover
- CTA opportunity

Mix of: educational, entertaining, promotional (80/20 rule)
```
